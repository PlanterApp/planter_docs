Looking for plant varieties with certain characteristics? Use variety tags to filter plant varieties and find seed companies offering those varieties! Or, filter plant varieties by your favorite seed companies.

{{< video mp4-src="recording.mp4" attributes="controls muted playsinline preload=metadata" >}}

1. **Open the Varieties Filter Menu**

   {{< screenshot 01-open-filter-menu.webp "Open Filter Menu" >}}

   Click the funnel-shaped "Filter" icon located at the top of the screen to open the filtering options.
2. **Filter by Seed Company**

   {{< screenshot 02-select-seeds-n-such-partner.webp "Select Seeds 'n Such Partner" >}}

   In the filter menu, select the checkbox next to the seed company to see which varieties that seed company offers (for the selected plant- tomatoes in this example. Companies listed under the "Planter Partners" section have an affiliate agreement with Planter.
3. **Filter By Variety Characteristics**

   {{< screenshot 03-select-determinate-growth-habit.webp "Select Determinate Growth Habit" >}}

   You can now filter varieties by their characteristics to find the perfect varieties for your garden! The applicable variety characteristics will vary from plant to plant. In this example, we expanded the "Growth Habit" options then selected "Determinate". You can filter for as many characteristics as you like!
4. **See Variety Details and Buy Seeds**

   {{< screenshot 04-purchase-rutgers-seeds.webp "Purchase Rutgers Seeds" >}}

   Scroll down the list of filtered varieties to find a variety of interest. Click the downward-pointing arrow icon next to its description to expand the full details. Click the shopping cart icon associated with the variety to see the seed company sources for that variety.
5. **Navigate to the Seed Company Website**

   {{< screenshot 05-navigate-to-seeds-n-such-website.webp "Navigate to Seeds 'n Such Website" >}}

   In the "Purchase Seeds" pop-up, click a seed company name to navigate to the external vendor's website.
