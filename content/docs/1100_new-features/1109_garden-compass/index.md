Use the garden compass to indicate the orientation of your garden, relative to the north.

Set the orientation manually or by pointing your device compass in the same direction as the top of your garden on the screen.

1. **Open the Garden Layers Menu**

   {{< screenshot 01-open-main-menu.webp "Open Main Menu" >}}

    In the garden view, tap the layers menu in the upper-right of the screen.
2. **Access Compass Settings**

   {{< screenshot 02-access-compass-settings.webp "Access Compass Settings" >}}

   From the open main menu, click on the 'Compass' option to open the 'Garden Orientation' settings dialog.
3. **Manually Adjust Orientation**

   {{< screenshot 03-manually-adjust-orientation.webp "Manually Adjust Orientation" >}}

   To manually set your garden’s orientation, drag the slider labeled 'Manually Adjust Orientation' to rotate the compass needle.
4. **Use Device Compass**

   {{< screenshot 04-enable-device-compass.webp "Enable Device Compass" >}}

   Toggle the 'Use Device Compass' switch to the 'on' position (green) to automatically set the garden's orientation using your device's built-in compass. Point your device to align it with the orientation of your garden.

5. **Save Garden Orientation**

   {{< screenshot 05-save-garden-orientation.webp "Save Garden Orientation" >}}

   Click the 'Save Orientation' button at the bottom of the dialog to apply the selected compass settings to your garden layout.

6. **Re-open Main Menu**

   {{< screenshot 06-re-open-main-menu.webp "Re-open Main Menu" >}}

   The compass will appear in the lower left corner of the screen. The compass will show your garden’s orientation relative to the north.

7. **Hide Compass Display**

   {{< screenshot 07-hide-compass-display.webp "Hide Compass Display" >}}

   If you want to hide the compass display, uncheck the checkbox next to the 'Compass' option in the menu. Your orientation settings will remain so you can enable it again later if needed.
