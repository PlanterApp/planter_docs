Now it’s easier than ever to multi-select garden squares. Simply tap two opposing corners and all squares in between will be selected!

Note: Corner multi-select only works for rectangular areas.

{{< video mp4-src="recording.mp4" attributes="controls muted playsinline preload=metadata" >}}

---

1. **Open the Garden Square Menu**

   {{< screenshot 01-open-the-garden-square-menu.webp "Open the Garden Square Menu" >}}

   Tap the garden square selector (third option from the right).

2. **Activate the Corner Selector**

   {{< screenshot 02-select-first-corner-of-bed.webp "Select First Corner of Bed" >}}

   Tap the corner multi-selector option (third option from the left).

3. **Select the First Corner**

   {{< screenshot 03-select-opposite-corner-of-bed.webp "Select Opposite Corner of Bed" >}}

   Tap the top-left corner of the desired square to initiate the selection of the planting area, then tap the bottom-right corner to complete the multi-square selection. Once the squares are selected, you can add plants, add notes (coming soon!) or customize the background and foreground.
