Planter now has the option to show either Variety labels or Plant and Variety labels in the garden view.

{{< video mp4-src="recording.mp4" attributes="controls muted playsinline preload=metadata" >}}

---

1. **Open the Garden Layers Menu**

   {{< screenshot 01-select-variety-for-labels.webp "Select 'Variety' for Labels" >}}

   In the garden view, tap the layers menu in the upper-right of the screen.

2. **Tap on Plant labels**

   {{< screenshot 02-tap-plant-labels-option.webp "Tap Plant Labels Option" >}}

   Tap on the 'Plant labels' option in the menu to expand the label display choices.
3. **Select 'Plant & Variety' for Labels**

   {{< screenshot 03-select-plant-variety-for-labels.webp "Select 'Plant & Variety' for Labels" >}}

   From the sub-menu, tap either ‘Variety’ or 'Plant & Variety' to display variety labels or plant and variety labels in the garden. Tap ‘None’ to remove the labels.
