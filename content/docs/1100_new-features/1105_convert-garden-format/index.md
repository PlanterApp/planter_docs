Convert your existing garden plans to the new Gardens 2.0 Format to take advantage of all the new garden features!

1. **Open Garden “Three Dots” Menu**

   {{< screenshot 01-open-backyard-garden-menu.webp "Open Backyard Garden Menu" >}}

   On the Gardens screen, tap the three vertical dots located to the right of the garden you wish to convert.
2. **Select Convert to New Format**

   {{< screenshot 02-select-convert-to-new-format.webp "Select Convert to New Format" >}}

   In the menu that appears, scroll down and tap the "Convert to new format" option.
3. **Confirm Garden Conversion**

   {{< screenshot 03-confirm-garden-conversion.webp "Confirm Garden Conversion" >}}

   A "Convert to new format?" dialog box will appear. If you wish to migrate Notes and Events to Tasks, ensure the "Migrate notes and events to Tasks" checkbox is selected. Tap the green "Convert" button to proceed.
4. **Convert from Within a Garden**

   {{< screenshot 04-access-garden-options-menu.webp "Access Garden Options Menu" >}}

   To convert garden format from within an existing garden, tap the three vertical dots located in the top right corner of the screen, then select “Convert to new format” from the menu options.
